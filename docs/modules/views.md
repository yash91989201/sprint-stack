# Views

## Purpose

Views give teams different ways to visualize, navigate, and act on issues depending on the task at hand — from focused lists to board-based workflows and time-oriented planning surfaces.

## Capabilities

- Kanban Board
- List View
- Timeline
- Calendar
- Roadmap
- My Work
- Backlog
- Archive

## Notes

Views draw from issues, status workflow, labels, milestones, and filters. Kanban and List are the primary day-to-day surfaces; Timeline, Calendar, Roadmap, and Archive support planning and historical review.
