# Workspaces

## Purpose
Workspaces are the top-level organizational container in Sprint Stack. A workspace holds members, projects, teams, and all associated work.

## Capabilities

- Create multiple workspaces
- Workspace logo, icon and branding
- Invite members
- Member directory
- Teams
- Roles & permissions
- Archived workspaces
- Favorites
- Workspace switching

## Notes

- [Teams](./teams.md) live inside a workspace and can be assigned to projects and issues.
- All [Projects](./projects.md) and [Issues](./issues.md) are scoped to a workspace.
- Workspace-level labels feed into the [Labels](./labels.md) module.
