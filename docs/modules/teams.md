# Teams

## Purpose
Teams group workspace members into collaboration units so work can be assigned, planned, and balanced by group rather than by individual.

## Capabilities

- Team profiles
- Team leads
- Team members
- Team issue assignment
- Team workload

## Notes

- Teams belong to a [Workspace](./workspaces.md).
- Projects and [Issues](./issues.md) can be assigned to a team as well as individual members.
- Team workload connects to [Reporting](./reporting.md) and planning views such as [Roadmaps](./roadmaps.md).
